kendo.ui.ExtDialog extends kendo.ui.Window
Introduced in 2013 Q1
Description
The ExtDialog widget positions buttons centered and along the bottom of the window. The buttons remain centered and along the bottom of the window even when the window is resized. To prevent the content from covering the buttons, the content is placed in a div and a scrollbar is displayed as needed.
